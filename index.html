About the Mehndi

Join us for a vibrant and joyous Mehndi ceremony filled with traditional henna art, music, and dancing. This beautiful pre-wedding celebration is all about community, joy, and the artistry of intricate henna designs.

What to Expect:

  • Traditional music and dancing
  • Professional henna artists
  • Delicious appetizers and refreshments
  • Beautiful photo opportunities
  • Traditional games and activities

Note: Please arrive on time as the henna application takes time. We recommend wearing clothes that you don't mind getting henna on!
