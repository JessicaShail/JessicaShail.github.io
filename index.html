Our Wedding Celebrations

J&S Wedding Weekend Celebrations

You are invited to join us for two days of blended traditions to celebrate this milestone in our relationship. Day 1 includes an afternoon Mehndi event. Day 2 includes a mid-afternoon ceremony and evening reception.

We are looking forward to bringing our communities together and sharing moments with you during this two-day event.

J&S Wedding Weekend Hopes

During this weekend our hope is that each one of you is all able to fully enjoy the experiences that we've planned including connecting with others and eating delicious food.

After the weekend has concluded our hope is that you remember a joyful experience and a sense of community (and that you liked the food).

What to Wear

J&S Wedding Weekend Attire

Colorful & Celebratory

We invite you to lean in to colour -- with the specific request that no one dress in all white, or in all black.

Day 1 (Saturday Mehndi)

Day 1 will be less formal. Feel free to wear something more casual (though you're welcome to dress up if you like). For those who would like to have henna applied to their hands/arms, please wear clothing that allows for this and know that your clothing may brush up against the paste and be stained.

Day 2 (Sunday Ceremony & Reception)

Day 2 is for dressing up a bit. We anticipate that guests will wear the same outfit for both Day 2 events. Brightly coloured saris, suits, dress shirts, pantsuits, dresses...your choice! There will be extended times when guests will be seated and there will be a dance lesson (some of you already know about Garba, the rest of you will have the chance to learn).

Remember: We want you to feel comfortable and celebrate with color! Avoid all white or all black outfits, but otherwise express yourself with bright, joyful colors.
